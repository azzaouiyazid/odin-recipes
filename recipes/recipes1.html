sky island specialty fruit sky seafood

  • 6 scallops (sashimi style)
  • 10 shrimp (shell on)
  • 1 grapefruit
  • 1 stalk celery
  • ½(100g) purple onion
  • 1 piece (10g) ginger
  • dash white pepper
  • ½tsp salt
  • olive oil (as desired)

how to cook :

  1. Boil the scallops 30 minutes. Then place into cold water (A). Cut in half lengthwise. Boil the shrimp in the shells. While they are still warm, remove the shells (B). If they are large, cut in half. Peel the outside peel and thin inner skin off the grapefruit. Cut the celery diagonally across the fibers. Slice the purple onion thinly. Slice the ginger.

  2. Put the scallops and shrimp in a bowl with lime juice, salt and pepper. Mix. Mix in onion, celery and ginger. Add grapefruit and refrigerate.

  3. After mixing once more, place in a serving dish and garnish with celery leaves. Add olive oil as desired.
