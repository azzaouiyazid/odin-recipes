gan falls pumpkin juice.

  • ~1/4 (300g) pumpkin
  • 300mL water
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar (or more as desired)
  • milk (as needed)

how to cook :

  1. Remove pumpkin seeds and skin. Cut into 1 cm thick slices. Put in a small pot, add water, and simmer over medium heat. When it becomes soft, remove from heat, and cool

  2. Add the first ingredients to a mixer. Add honey, and sugar, mix. Then chill in refrigerator

  3. Pour the second ingredients into a glass. Add milk as desired. Drink while mixing.
